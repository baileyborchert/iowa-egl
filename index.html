About Us

Welcome to Iowa EGL & J-Fashion! We're a community founded in 2007 to bring together Iowans who share a love for Japanese lolita fashion. Since then, we've also expanded our community to include other Japanese alternative fashion styles. Our purpose is to organize events, talk about fashion, buy or sell items, and make friends.

We host meetups in person once or twice a month, on average. Events are typically dress-coded, but in our online spaces, we welcome everyone from J-Fashion veterans to beginners. If you're interested in becoming a member and keeping up-to-date with the latest happenings, please join our Discord!
